A Tribute to Jennifer Aniston

Jennifer Joanna Aniston (born February 11, 1969) is an American actress, film producer, and businesswoman. The daughter of actors John Aniston and Nancy Dow

Jennifer

Born - She was born in Sherman Oaks, Los Angeles.Her parents are the actors John Aniston and Nancy Dow. She had two half-brothers named Alex Aniston and John Melick. As a child she lived in Greece a year with her family. Then they moved to Eddystone, Pennsylvania and then to New York City.
School - Jennifer went to school at Rudolf Steiner School in New York. She then graduated from Manhattan's Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts. in 1987.
Movies - She apeared in her first movie in 1997. It was called Picture Perfect. She made 2,000,000 dollars by doing this movie. In the movie she was the main character Kate that had to deal with balancing work and her love life.
